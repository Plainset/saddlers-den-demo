Ordering & bridle measuring

Contact the workshop with details of the item you'd like and, if needed, sizes. Because every piece is handmade, price and lead time are confirmed once we know what's required; postage is only confirmed once your item is made and packed.

For a made-to-measure bridle, local customers can have measurements taken in person at the workshop; postal customers can request the measuring form by phone or email. Free adjustments apply to any bridle measured this way.

FAQ

Do you publish prices for saddlery and leather goods?
Not as fixed prices — every bridle, saddle, harness set and leather good is made to order, so we quote once we know the specification. Leatherwork and saddle-fitting courses do have fixed, published prices; see the Services page.
How long does a bespoke order take?
It depends on the piece and current workshop workload — we'll give you an estimated timeframe with your quote.
Can I get a saddle fitted rather than ordering blind?
Yes — the workshop offers a saddle fitting service alongside custom-measured saddles and GFS (Fieldhouse Riding) saddles with interchangeable head plates.
Do I need experience to book a leatherwork course?
No — the Introduction to Leatherwork and Basic Leatherwork courses are designed for beginners. Intermediate Leatherwork and the Saddle Flocking & Adjustment courses assume prior experience or completion of Part 1.
