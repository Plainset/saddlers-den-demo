## Design Notes
- Palette: Oxblood/burgundy (#6b1f2a) drawn from the business's own logo mark, warm saddle-tan (#b5793a), cream parchment background (#f6efe4), near-black ink (#231a14) for body text, brass/gold accent (#a9822c) for small details (rules, icons). Distinct from generic equestrian-green palettes used elsewhere.
- Typography: serif display face (e.g. "Playfair Display"/"Georgia" fallback) for headings to match the heraldic seal branding; clean sans body (system-ui) for readability.
- Image layout pattern: mixed — wrapper `aspect-ratio` + `object-fit: cover` for larger product shots (shooting-bags, satchels, watchbands, rifle-case, dog-collar, rifle-vintage), native `img` `aspect-ratio` + `height:auto` for the two small equestrian shots (bridle-horse/saddle-horse at native 302x331) to avoid upscaling past ~1.3x.
- Risk notes: bridle-horse.jpg and saddle-horse.jpg are small natives (302x331) — capped to a max display width (~340px) in CSS so upscale ratio stays under 1.3x; never used full-bleed. rifle-vintage.jpg is the only image judged strong enough for a full-bleed/large hero placement (composition, lighting, genuine craft moment); all others used as inset/card images.
